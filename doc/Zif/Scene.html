Overview

A Scene is a full-screen view of your game. The concept is to show one scene at a time. So each scene in your game should be a subclass of Zif::Scene which overrides #perform_tick. Using the structure in Zif::Game, #perform_tick comes after input handling and before updating Actionables. So your subclass should use #perform_tick to add/remove Clickables/Actionables, and respond to any detected input. Switching scenes is handled in Zif::Game, based on the return value of #perform_tick.
